Services

Hospitals offer a comprehensive array of services to patients, encompassing emergency care for acute conditions, surgical interventions to address diseases or injuries, diagnostic imaging and laboratory testing for accurate diagnosis, inpatient and outpatient care for various medical needs, intensive care for critical conditions, maternity services for prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care, rehabilitation to aid recovery and regain functionality, mental health services for psychological support and treatment, and preventive care to promote wellness and prevent illness. These services are delivered by skilled healthcare professionals and supported by advanced technology, aiming to provide holistic care and improve patient outcomes.

Emergency care

The Emergency Department of goddoc World Hospital, This purpose-built facility, with state-of-the-art equipment is for people who need immediate hospital attention due to serious illness or injury. We provide a 24 hour, 7 day a week consultant-led service to the people of Bangalore and surrounding communities, seeing around 1500 patients a month.

Maternity services

Maternity services encompass prenatal care, childbirth assistance, and postpartum support provided by healthcare professionals including obstetricians, midwives, and nurses. These services aim to ensure the well-being of both mother and baby throughout pregnancy, labor, delivery, and the postnatal period, addressing medical needs and offering guidance and education.

Patient Services

Patient services encompass a spectrum of care and support provided by healthcare facilities, including appointment scheduling, medical billing, health education, and assistance with insurance. These services prioritize patient comfort, convenience, and satisfaction, aiming to enhance the overall healthcare experience and optimize outcomes.

Laboratory testing

Laboratory testing involves analyzing bodily samples such as blood, urine, or tissue to diagnose diseases, monitor health conditions, or assess treatment effectiveness. Utilizing advanced technology and methodologies, laboratories provide accurate results that guide medical decision-making, enabling timely intervention and personalized patient care.

Intensive care

Intensive care units (ICUs) provide specialized medical care for critically ill patients requiring close monitoring and life-saving interventions. Staffed by skilled healthcare professionals and equipped with advanced technology, ICUs deliver intensive support for organ function, ventilation, and stabilization, aiming to optimize patient outcomes and survival.

Surgery

Surgery involves invasive procedures performed by trained surgeons to treat diseases, injuries, or abnormalities. Utilizing precision instruments and techniques, surgery aims to remove, repair, or modify affected tissues or organs, restoring function and alleviating symptoms to improve the patient's health and quality of life.
